Conductors

At the Barrier Reef Orchestra we have many talented conductors who perform at our many different concerts.

Current Conductors

  • Mark Shiell

    • Mark has guest-conducted at the Melbourne Symphony Orchestra, Moscow Symphony Orchestra, Symphony Orchestra of St. Petersburg and the West Australian Symphony to just name a few. He currently servers as the Artistic Director and Principal Conductor at the Zelman Symphony Orchestra, Ballarat Symphony and the Macquarie Philharmonia.

  • Raymond Yong

    • A Malaysian born conductor, he had emigrated to Australia at a young age. Conducting during his school years, he became the Music Director of the Victorian Youth Symphony Orchestra, Victroia Choarle and the founding Director of the Orchestra at age 21.

  • Richard McIntyre

    • Richard has maintained a long career as a bassoonist, music educator, conductor and an arranger. He was appointed as Associate Principal Bassoon with the Sydney Symphony Orchestra when he was 20 in 1968, then moving to The Canberra School of Music as a founding member of the Canberra Wind Soloists in 1978.

  • Mario Dobernig

    • Mario grew up in a small Austrian Alps village before starting his professional career as a percussionist in Graz, Austria and Helsinki, Finland. He has graduated from the Art University Graz and the Sibelius Academy of Helsinki, Mario also holds a Masters degree in Musicology from the University of Helsinki.
