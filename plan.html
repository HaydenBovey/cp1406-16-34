CP1406 - 2016 Group Project Plan

Group Number 34

Members: Nicola Webster, Philip Woodward, Hayden Bovey, Darren Stoneman

Goals

  • To have 5% fewer empty seats at each concert
  • To attract 2% additional younger people to attend each concert
  • To produce a static website that is: more appealing and inviting than the existing one
  • uses forms with Javascript error checking
  • educational on multiple levels

Success Evaluation

  • Count empty seats, divide by total number of seats, * by 100 for %
  • use line graph to view annual empty seat % trends
  • Roughly count the number of people under 25 and express as a % of the total audience. Graph.
  • ask usability testers to rate the new and old websites on appeal and ease of use (if possible get feedback from client)
  • have forms tested in usability tests to ensure they are clear, easy to use and produce the correct information

Target Audience

  • Existing concert goers
  • Students (and their families) studying music (learning instruments) at Townsville schools
  • University students
